I'm a first-generation college graduate and professional, raised in a hardworking immigrant family where resilience and discipline were part of everyday life. I worked my way through college at UC Davis, never imagining that my career would eventually lead me to where I am today.


I began my career in event planning before transitioning into tech as an Executive Assistant at Affirm, and later at Cisco Meraki. What started as executive support quickly evolved into program management and operations, where I partnered closely with global leaders across technical support, customer success, and escalations teams to design onboarding programs, operating frameworks, and systems that helped the organization scale with clarity and efficiency.


While working at Meraki, a data-heavy project sparked my interest in learning how to code. I realized that a deeper technical understanding would allow me to design more efficient and scalable solutions, so I began teaching myself programming fundamentals. That curiosity led to an intensive bootcamp, followed by an apprenticeship where I gained hands on experience, and eventually a full-time role as a Software Engineer on Meraki’s award winning Dashboard API team.


Outside of my professional work, health and fitness have always been a constant in my life. I’m a former certified personal trainer and remain deeply invested in wellness, strength, and sustainable routines. I’m currently spending time on a few early passion projects, including a fitness app. I’m also a wife and mom to two young kids, and I love exploring new places with my little family, trying new recipes, and finding balance between ambition and everyday life.
